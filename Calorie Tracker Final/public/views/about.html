    What does this web app do?

Our web app will contain a search bar where our client is able type in the food of their choice. Once they press "ENTER", a list of potential meals/dishes that match their search will pop up. Once the client selects an option, they will be able to input a quantity and keep track of their total calories. Our app will display nutrition information such as calories, fat, and sugar. They can then add each food to their total, which will keep track of each specific food and display the client's total calorie count and macros.
